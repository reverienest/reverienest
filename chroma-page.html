Chroma - The Start of a Journey

My first game. Chroma is a simple platformer in which the player must avoid obstacles as they run across the clouds before time runs out. As my first foray into game development, much of development was a learning process, that being the single night we had to complete it, the culmination of a previous failed project leading up to a deadline for a school comptetition. it was a fun project, during which I created the game's systems and levels, learning the ins and outs of game development along the way.
