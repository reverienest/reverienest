Previous Projects:
Some things I've worked in in the past

Project Chronograph (2018-2019)

A time-bending platformer in which the player must possess objects and manipulate their movements using an ancient device that controls time. I worked on this project with a team of my peers in my freshman year of college using Unity. Super fun experience!

Piecewise (2015-2016)

A short RPG-horror game that tells the story of two sisters and strength of a sibling bond. Made in RPGMaker as an experiment with a group of friends. I got some great experience in project management and team colaboration!

Chroma (2014)

The first game I ever made, Chroma is a small plaformer in which the player travels through the islands of Chroma, racing against time to prevent the Dusk from spreading across her village. I made this in a single night for a school competition after deliberating for weeks. This would be my first step into the realm of Game Development.
