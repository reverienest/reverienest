Something About Me...

Games are a place for those without one, or so they say. As someone whose childhood was spent exploring fantastical worlds behind a digial screen, I can't help but agree. With each playthrough I learn a little bit more about myself, about the world I live in, and the people around me. Every closing screen leaves me feeling just a little more confident in returning to reality. With that in mind, I'll be putting everything I have into creating experiences of my own design, in the hopes that the player might be able to leave feeling the same as I do.

I'm a Game Designer & Producer.
